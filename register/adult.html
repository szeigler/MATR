Adult Twin Registration

We want to make registering with the MATR as convenient for you as possible. In order to do so, we provide a few options you can choose from to send us your registration request.

Request registration:

You may fill-out the form below with the requested information and click the “Submit” button when you are done.

If you prefer, you may contact the MATR by phone (1-800-872-8946), email (matr@vcu.edu), or mail (PO Box 980617 Richmond, VA 23298) and provide us with the information requested below.

Once we receive your request for registration with the MATR, one of our research interviewers will contact you by phone or mail a registration packet to you, depending upon your preference. Thanks in advance for registering with the MATR!
