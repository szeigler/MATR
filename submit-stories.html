SUBMIT STORIES

The MATR loves hearing from our twins, so if you have a story to tell, feel free to send it to us!

Stories that are submitted to us may be selected for us in our newsletters, website, or other MATR publications. So, you just might see your story in our next newsletter!

In order to publish your story, we will need to obtain permission to do so. For stories about adult twins, we'll need both twins to sign the Story Permission Form. For stories about juvenile twins, we'll need the parent who submits the story to sign the Story Permision Form.
